About

I'm Daven and this page attempts to reduce my life down to a single page of text. Here goes...

I was a teacher in Japan for 8 years, but ultimately changed my career to a software engineer. Here's how I did it.

I spent my childhood doing what amounts to the following three things:

  • playing with computers
  • playing Dance Dance Revolution
  • playing video games

In elementary school, I was building computers for friends and family. By high school I was using OllyDb to create my own rules within the games I enjoyed.

I graduated from the University of Waterloo, where I got my butt kicked by the course curriculum. By sheer luck, I got an offer to come to Japan for the JET programme and left everything I knew to come. Little did I know, I would still be here 10 years later.
